Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to our most common questions.

How long does it take to build a website? ▼
A basic website typically takes 3–7 working days. More complex websites with e-commerce or custom features may take 2–4 weeks. We keep you updated throughout the process.
What do I need to register a SIM card? ▼
You will need a valid Ghana Card (National ID). We also accept passport and other official ID documents for non-citizens. The registration process takes only a few minutes.
What is the minimum amount for mobile money transactions? ▼
The minimum transaction amount depends on the network but is typically GH₵ 1. We handle both small and large transactions efficiently and securely.
Do you offer IT training for beginners? ▼
Yes! Our IT training is designed for all levels — from complete beginners to intermediate users. We start with the basics and progress at your own pace. No prior experience needed.
Can I get a website with no hosting fees? ▼
Yes! We help you deploy your website on free platforms like GitHub Pages, Netlify, or Vercel. For more advanced features, we'll guide you to affordable hosting options.
How can I pay for your services? ▼
We accept Mobile Money (MTN MoMo, Telecel Cash, AirtelTigo Money) and cash payments. For websites, a 50% deposit is required upfront with the balance upon completion.
